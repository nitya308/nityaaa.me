Student VolunteerMatch

finding service projects for students • 2019-2021

The Solution

Student VolunteerMatch was mid-lockdown website to help students find the perfect community service project for them. I designed and coded this one myself! It displays a customisation engine with a quiz that takes in the user’s interests, skills and time frame and outputs personalised recommendations of volunteer projects in the area for them. At each stage they can select as many areas of interest as they like. You can check out the website here.

Coding skills: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React

Design skills: very basic ones, I self-learned some UI-UX design off the internet!

↑ A section of the matching quiz

The Structure

I asked students at my high school what criteria they filter projects by. The most common answers I got were: area of interest, the skills they need to do the work and time commitment! (Location was another factor but since this was covid times, everything was on zoom-street). Based on this, I decided to filter at 3 stages.

  1. Interests: this eliminates the most non-relevant projects for the user at the first stage, since most projects only fall under 1-2 interest categories. Handle multidimensional service proects that fall under multiple interests.
  2. Skills: each project also has listed needs from lots of different skills ranging from writing, graphic design, to editing, tutoring etc.
  3. Time commitment: this is the last filter because it eliminates the least projects (since most projects are flexible with time commitments).
